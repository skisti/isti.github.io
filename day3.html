Journey from Manali will start in the morning by SUV or Tampo travellers depend on the size of the group. This will around 12 hrs to reach Kaza.

The first stop will be a mighty Rohtang Pass which is one of the oldest pass. Known for its scenic beauty, Rohtang Pass holds strategic importance for India. It's the gateway to Keylong in the landlocked Lahaul Valley from Manali. Spend some time at the pass and then head towards Gramphoo for breakfast.

From Gramphoo you shall start noticing change in terrain where you will see less trees and more of steep mountain peaks and beginning of barren landscape.

From here we will head towards Chatru, you will start noticing changes in terrain and face some of the biggest waterfalls crossing the road. Moving forward you will see rocks all over with huge streams crossing the road and converging in the ruthless Chandra River.

We will stop at Batalfor lunch break and then head to Kunzum Pass one of the highest motorable mountain pass of India.The jumpy ride with non-existent road which started from Gramphoo will end in Lohsarwhich is adjacent to Indo-Chinese border. Here we will have a small break and just enjoy the mesmerizing view of Lohsar the first inhabited view of Spiti Village.

It will take another 2 hours of this beautiful journeyto end and finally we will reach Kaza which is a main city and headquarter of Spiti Valley. Over here we will check-in a hotel and enjoy a cup of tea. We shall have a small briefing session at dinner table.This tiring day will end with a gaze at the gorgeous sky of a million stars and a prominent milky way.
