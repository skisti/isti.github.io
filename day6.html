After Breakfast with the locals, head towards Chandrataal Lake. This is another location where you will find it hard to stop clicking photographs.

Marvel at the lake changing its appearance almost with every passing hour, each look as stunning as the other.

Tonight camp in proper adventure style; excellent dome tents set up to protect you from the cold and biting night winds.
