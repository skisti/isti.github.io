Its 12 to 14hours journey depends on the weather and monsoon. We will Reach Manali in the morning and check in to the hotel. This day will be open and you are free to do whatever you want but most important thing is to relax.

There are various place to visit in Manali, you can visit Hidimba Temple which date backs to 1553 AD or Manu temple which is only accessible by foot or Vashisht Temple to have a bath in hot spring, or just explore some good café with continental food in Old Manali.

In case you have missed packing something then this is the time when you should go shopping as Spiti valley doesn’t have such luxuries.
